Welcome to the Manager Support Hub

This site is aimed to help managers, supervisors and administrators manage staff in ESR. We have included direct links to the actions in ESR to save you from having to search through the menus, a quick guide, the full user guides and help and advice on managing staff.

We hope you find this page useful and informative. Please feel free to explore the different sections and links. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please let us know. Additional Supervisor

You can delegate management responsibilities to another manager or administrator if you are going to be unavailable¬†

NHS TEWV
  1. Click here and click Action next to the team member you would like to update.
  2. Click Add.
  3. Click the Magnifying glass icon and enter the person you want to delegate responsibilities to.
  4. Enter a From Date. To Date is optional.
  5. Click Apply.

Change Location

It is essential that staff location/base is correct in ESR as this has an impact on travel claims.

Please note if you cannot find the location in the list please contact tewv.wfi@nhs.net who will be able to help

NHS TEWV
  1. Click here and click Action next to the team member you would like to update.
  2. Select the date you want the location to change from and click Continue.
  3. Click the Magnifying glass icon, enter the new location, and click Go.
  4. Select the Location.
  5. Add a Change Reason and click Submit.

For the full user guide, please click here.

Change Supervisor

You must be the employee‚Äôs current supervisor to change the supervisor to someone else. If you are not the current supervisor then please speak to the current supervisor or contact tewv.wfi@nhs.net

NHS TEWV

Declaration of Interest

Declarations of interest are no longer added in Civica Declare and instead added in ESR. Its important to note that there are various actions and new guides have been developed. Please note you must have either Manager or Supervisor Self Service access to manage declarations, but all staff can add a declaration.

NHS TEWV
  1. Click Here and then click Add Declaration
  2. Select the category and situation
  3. Enter a description
  4. Select the date from and date to if relevant
  5. Add any comments
  6. Click apply

If you have no interests to declare click this at the top of the page.
